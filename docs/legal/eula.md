## 1. What Fawx Is

Fawx is a desktop AI agent application for macOS and iOS. It runs locally on your device, connects to third-party AI providers (such as Anthropic and OpenAI) using your own API keys or subscriptions, and optionally connects to a fleet of your own machines over Tailscale.

Fawx consists of two parts:

- **The Kernel** — proprietary, compiled binary distributed as-is, code-signed and notarized by Apple.
- **The Skill SDK and Loadable Layer** — open-source components licensed separately under the Apache License 2.0.
