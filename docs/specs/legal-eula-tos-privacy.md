## Privacy Policy — Key Points

### What We Collect: NOTHING by default

Fawx runs entirely locally. By default:
- **No telemetry** — disabled by default, opt-in only
- **No analytics** — no tracking pixels, no usage analytics
- **No cloud storage** — all data stays on your device
- **No account required** — Fawx works without creating an account with us
- **No API key transmission** — keys are encrypted locally, never sent to us

### If Telemetry Is Enabled (opt-in)

When you explicitly enable telemetry, we collect:
- **Tool usage** — which tools succeed/fail and how often (not what files or content)
- **Proposal gate** — how often the safety gate fires (not what was blocked)
- **Experiments** — scores and decisions (not code content)
- **Errors** — error categories and frequency (messages are hashed, not sent in cleartext)
- **Model usage** — which models and thinking levels (not conversations)
- **Performance** — response times and token counts (not prompts or responses)

What we NEVER collect, even with telemetry enabled:
- Conversation content, prompts, or responses
- File contents or paths
- API keys, tokens, or credentials
- IP addresses (session IDs are random, reset on restart)
- Device identifiers or hardware fingerprints
- Personal information

### Third-Party Data Sharing
- **None.** We don't sell, share, or provide telemetry data to third parties.
- If that ever changes, it will require a new opt-in consent flow.

### Data Retention
- Telemetry signals are buffered in memory only (not persisted to disk)
- If you restart Fawx, the buffer is cleared
- If you disable telemetry, all buffered signals are immediately deleted
- If/when we add upload capability (future), we will implement:
  - Differential privacy (aggregation before storage)
  - 90-day rolling retention
  - User-accessible data export (right to access)
  - User-accessible data deletion (right to erasure)

### Third-Party AI Providers
- When you use Anthropic, OpenAI, or other providers through Fawx, your prompts and responses are subject to those providers' privacy policies
- We don't see or store that data — it flows directly between your device and the provider
- OAuth tokens for ChatGPT subscriptions are stored in your local encrypted credential store

### Children
- Fawx is not directed at children under 13
- We do not knowingly collect data from children

### Changes
- We will notify users of material privacy policy changes through the app
- Continued use after notification = acceptance
