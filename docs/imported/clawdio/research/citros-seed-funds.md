# Citros Research: Seed-Stage Investor Targets

**Research Date:** February 11, 2026  
**Target:** Seed-stage investors for Citros — a consumer AI phone agent for Android

---

## About Citros

**What:** Native Android app that gives AI (Claude, GPT, local models) actual control of your phone via Accessibility Services  
**Stage:** Working MVP, pre-revenue, pre-launch  
**Category:** Consumer AI, mobile AI agent, AI automation  
**Distribution:** Sideload-only (Play Store won't allow Accessibility Service automation)  
**Tech:** Kotlin Android, multi-provider AI (Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter), structured tool calling

## Investment Landscape Summary (2025-2026)

**Key Trends:**
- AI startups grabbed 34% of all VC funding in 2025 while representing only 18% of funded companies
- Seed-stage AI startups receive valuations ~42% higher than non-AI peers
- Private AI investment reached $33.9B in 2024 (up 18.7% YoY, 8.5x higher than 2022)
- US dominates: $109.1B in private AI investment (2024) vs China's $9.3B

**Typical Seed Check Ranges:**
- Pre-seed AI: $500K–$2M (vs $250K–$1M non-AI)
- Seed AI: $1M–$5M
- Traditional seed: $500K–$3M

**Application Strategy:**
1. **Prioritize warm introductions** through founder networks, advisors, or portfolio connections
2. **Target 3-5 firms per category** based on thesis fit and portfolio alignment
3. **Emphasize technical moats** — AI models, accessibility service integration, multi-provider architecture
4. **Address distribution challenge upfront** — sideload-only may concern some VCs, but others will see it as avoiding platform risk
5. **Highlight consumer defensibility** — why users will stick with Citros vs competitors
6. **Consider accelerators first** (YC, Techstars) for credibility + network if pre-revenue

**Red Flags to Address:**
- Distribution risk (no Play Store)
- Consumer AI adoption timeline
- Privacy concerns (Accessibility Services)
- Competitive moat vs future OS-level features

**Green Flags to Highlight:**
- Working MVP (technical de-risking)
- Multi-AI provider strategy (not locked to one model)
- Android-first (less crowded than iOS AI agents)
- Novel UI/UX for phone control
- Clear path to monetization (subscription, freemium, etc.)
