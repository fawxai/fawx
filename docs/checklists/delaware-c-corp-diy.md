## Step 2: File Certificate of Incorporation ($89)

**Go to:** [icis.corp.delaware.gov/ecorp/](https://icis.corp.delaware.gov/ecorp/)

**Click:** "Incorporate a Business Entity" → "General Corporation"

**Fill in:**
- **Corporation Name:** Fawx AI, Inc.
- **Registered Agent:** (from Step 1)
- **Authorized Shares:** 10,000,000 shares of Common Stock, par value $0.00001 per share
  - (This is standard. High share count + low par value = flexibility for future equity grants and fundraising)
- **Incorporator:** Your name + your CO address (this is public record)

**Pay:** $89 (standard filing) or $189 (24-hour expedited) or $1,089 (same-day)
- Standard takes 3-5 business days
- 24-hour is worth the extra $100 if you want to ship this week

**You'll receive:** A filed Certificate of Incorporation with a file number

## Step 3: Get an EIN (free, immediate)

**Go to:** [irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online)

**Requirements:**
- SSN of the responsible party (you)
- Delaware mailing address (use your CO address — this is where IRS mail goes, not the registered agent)
- Entity type: Corporation
- Reason: Started a new business
- Date business started: (filing date from Step 2)

**You get the EIN immediately online.** Save the confirmation letter as PDF.

## Step 5: Initial Board Consent (free, 15 min)

**Action by Written Consent of the Sole Director of Fawx AI, Inc.**

This is a signed document where you (as sole director) resolve:

1. **Adopt bylaws** — "The bylaws attached hereto are adopted as the bylaws of the corporation."
2. **Appoint officers** — "Joseph [Last Name] is appointed as CEO, Secretary, and Treasurer."
3. **Authorize stock issuance** — "The corporation is authorized to issue [X] shares of Common Stock to [your name] at par value ($0.00001/share) in exchange for [consideration — typically assignment of pre-existing IP]."
4. **Approve fiscal year** — Calendar year.
5. **Authorize bank account** — "The CEO is authorized to open a corporate bank account."
6. **Adopt stock purchase agreement** — (see Step 6)

Sign it, date it, save it.

- [ ] Board consent signed

## Step 6: Issue Founder Stock (free, 15 min)

**Stock Purchase Agreement** between you and Fawx AI, Inc.

- **Shares:** 8,000,000 shares of Common Stock (80% of authorized — leaves room for future grants)
- **Price:** $0.00001 × 8,000,000 = $80.00 (write a check to the company, or just record it)
- **Consideration:** $80.00 cash + assignment of all pre-existing Fawx IP (code, designs, specs)
- **83(b) Election:** **FILE THIS WITHIN 30 DAYS.** Mail to IRS. This is the one thing you absolutely cannot forget — it prevents a massive tax bill if the company becomes valuable.

**83(b) election:** Print two copies, sign both, mail one to the IRS service center for your area (include a self-addressed stamped envelope for their copy back). Keep the other copy.

## Step 8: Register as Foreign Corp in Colorado (~$50)

Since you operate from CO but are incorporated in DE, you need to register as a "foreign corporation" in Colorado.

**Go to:** Colorado Secretary of State — [sos.state.co.us](https://www.sos.state.co.us/pubs/business/businessHome.html)

**File:** Statement of Foreign Entity Authority
**Fee:** ~$50
**You'll need:** DE file number, EIN, your CO address

- [ ] Colorado foreign corp registration filed

## Step 9: Ongoing Compliance (annual)

- **Delaware Annual Report + Franchise Tax:** Due March 1 each year. Minimum $400/yr for C-corps. (Yes, Delaware charges franchise tax even if you make $0.)
- **Colorado Periodic Report:** Due annually, ~$10.
- **Federal taxes:** File Form 1120 (corporate tax return) even if revenue is $0.
- **Registered agent renewal:** Whatever your agent charges annually.

## ⚠️ Critical Reminders

1. **83(b) election must be filed within 30 days of stock issuance.** This is the single most important thing. Miss this and you could owe taxes on the paper value of your shares when they vest.
2. **Delaware franchise tax is due March 1 every year.** Even if you make $0. Set a calendar reminder now.
3. **Keep all documents together** — Certificate of Incorporation, bylaws, board consents, stock ledger, 83(b) copy. These are your corporate records. Store digitally + print copies.
